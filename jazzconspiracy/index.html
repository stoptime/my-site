Jazz Conspiracy

Jazz Conspiracy was born out of the Bloom School of Jazz - where many of us met, studied, practiced - and eventually performed at the Jazz Showcase. We were an actively gigging/recording band from about 2003-2010 or so, being regularly featured for entire weekends at Pops for Champagne.

Below are some of my favorite tracks - you can listen to all of them for free over at SoundCloud.
